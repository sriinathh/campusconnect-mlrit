Sports

Sport is an integral part of the curriculum. Various sports facility is provided to the students within the campus. The college is committed to create a balanced atmosphere of academic, cultural and sports activities for the overall personality development of its students. Various sports competitions such as inter departmental, Inter collegiate, Inter University, etc help in developing team spirit in students. Sports and games help the students to improve their interpersonal relationship am in healthy manner. Talented students are honored with medals, trophies and certificates..

MLRIT Library

The MLR Institute of Technology (MLRIT) library offers a comprehensive range of resources and services to support the academic needs of its students and faculty.

Print Resources

  • Titles: 6,840
  • Volumes: 39,104
  • National Journals: 148
  • International Journals: 92

Digital Resources

  • Access to e-journals, e-books, and multimedia content through a digital library equipped with 30 computer systems
  • Membership with the Developing Library Network (DELNET) for inter-library loans and resource sharing
  • Access to NPTEL video lectures for enhanced learning
  • Subscription to N-LIST e-resources via INFLIBNET

Additional Facilities

  • Fully computerized system with bar-coding for efficient management
  • Photocopying services

Online Resources

  • Online Public Access Catalogue
  • JNTUH E-LSDM e-Learning
  • NPTEL Video Lectures
  • National Digital Library
